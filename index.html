Our Dental Specialist

Our team members are registered specialists with the Ministry of Health Malaysia and Malaysian Dental Council. Our aim is to provide high quality dental treatment in a friendly atmosphere. Our team use the most effective techniques available and follow the most recent infection control guidelines. We work together as a mutually supportive team.

Our Services

Periodontal Treatment

Periodontitis is inflammation which extends to destroy the deeper supporting tissues of the tooth eventually leading to tooth loss.

Dental Implant Treatment

Dental implants are usually made from titanium and are unique in forming a natural longstanding union with the jaw bone a process commonly described as osseointegration.

Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontics is the dental specialty which uses removable and fixed braces to straighten crooked teeth and improve the way they bite together.

Aesthetic Dental Treatment

Aesthetic Dentistry involves skills and techniques to improve your smile’s appearance as well as its overall health and function. It consists of teeth whitening and dental veneers (direct and indirect).

General Dental Treatment

General Dentistry ensures that you do not have any issues such as cavities or gum disease.
